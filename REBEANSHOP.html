Our Story

From Coffee Beans to Candle Scents

ReBean was born from a shared love of coffee's rich aroma and the cozy ambiance it creates. We believe that the comforting scent of coffee should be enjoyed beyond the cup.

Our journey began in a small workshop where we discovered how to capture the complex notes of coffee beans and transform them into long-lasting, captivating fragrances that transform any space into a warm sanctuary.
